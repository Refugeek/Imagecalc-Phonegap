Fiverr is a popular marketplace for microjobs, called "gigs".

This app allows Fiverr sellers to easily calculate the appropriate dimensions for their own gig images. It was created as a convenience for other Fiverr sellers by Refugeek.

Image requirements can change at any time. Don't sue me if the app messes up, I'm a poor seller just like you :)
